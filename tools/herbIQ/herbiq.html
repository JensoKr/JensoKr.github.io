Interactive tool · 2026

From a process-based simulation to your browser.

Based on my simulations with the Madingley & LPJ‑GUESS model system, I distilled a full trophic pyramid into a machine-learning ecosystem estimator using scikit-learn and py2app.

You can move the sliders to set herbivore biomass, abundance, climate and precipitation — three histogram-gradient-boosting regressor models predict net primary productivity, herbivory pressure and standing leaf biomass in real time, right in your browser.
