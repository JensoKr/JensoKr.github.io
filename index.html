About

Two practices, one curiosity — finding structure in complex systems.

By day I write code to further develop global vegetation models that incorporate the whole food‑web — to assess how biodiversity unfolds under changing boundary conditions.

By weekend I pack my Canon R5 and head into nature. The two worlds inform each other: both reward patience, attention to detail, and the willingness to wait for just the right moment.
