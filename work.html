Poster Presentation at European Conference of Ecological Modelling (ECEM 2023)
I presented my recent work progress concerning the Coupling of LPJ-GUESS and the Madingley model at the ECEM. I was amazed about the positiv feedback and the interactions with multiple other working groups, which are working on similar model development.
Organisation of the Friends of Madingley Meeting (FoM)
After attending the Friends of Madingley Meeting in 2019, me and my working group were keen to participate in another FoM Meeting. After the pandemic we decided to host the meeting at KIT in Garmisch-Partenkirchen. I drafted a schedule and organised the gathering. Together with Almut Arneth, I chaired the meeting.
Published Article in Ecological Modelling
We published my progress in the offline coupling between LPJ-GUESS and the Madingley model in Ecological Modelling Issue #471.

Presentation at World Biodiversity Conference in Davos
At the World Biodiversity Conference 2020 in Davos, I was keen to present the progess in driving the Madingley model with vegetation from LPJ-GUESS. This was the first big conference I ever visited and I was overwhelmed by the interesting podeum discussions of high-profile researchers. Equally exciting was the positive feedback and the pragmatical questions of the visitors during the poster session.
